Garage Bartender Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 30, 2026

Garage Bartender is an Alexa skill created for entertainment purposes. It provides welcome messages, closing-time messages, and jokes.

Information We Collect

Garage Bartender does not collect, store, sell, or share personal information.

The skill does not request access to your name, email address, location, phone number, contacts, payment information, or smart home device data.

Alexa and Amazon Data

When you use Garage Bartender, Amazon may process your voice request in order to operate Alexa and deliver the skill experience. That processing is handled by Amazon under Amazon’s own privacy policies and Alexa settings.

Skill Responses

Garage Bartender responds with random entertainment messages, such as greetings, jokes, and closing-time sendoffs. These responses are generated from a predefined list of messages.

Children’s Privacy

Garage Bartender is not directed toward children and does not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any updates will be posted on this page.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the skill developer through the support information provided in the Alexa skill listing.
